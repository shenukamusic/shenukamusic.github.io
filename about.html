SHENUKA JAYASINGHE

Shenuka is a concert pianist from Sri Lanka and currently studies under Prof. Murray McLachlan in the Royal Northern College of Music in Manchester, UK. He is an enthusiast of classical music improvisation and believes we all have the power to be creative in art. His YouTube channel is dedicated to bringing out and inspiring the creative spirit within each one of us, by improvising on anyone's ideas on the piano.

COMPOSER

Shenuka is a strong believer in the fact that in our pursuit for happinness there are two positive actions that can give us a strong sense of fulfillment and happiness. That is to love and to create.

He started his YouTube Channel to inspire the creative spirit that's within us all to create music. This creativity is especially apparent within children, where they do not care about what the world may think of their innovations or lack thereof; they just do what makes them happy. There is a lot that we can learn from them, and perhaps the channel may inspire someone to value the creative power inside them.

He is new to the world of composition as a pianist, and like the romantics, his compositions are closely tied into his life experiences.

ENSEMBLE DIRECTOR

Shenuka is the director of Musica Dei, a Roman Catholic Liturgical Choir. It is the only one of its kind in the UK, where they are newly bringing in (or technically because of its ancient history, brining back) the niche art of Corsican liturgical chant. It is unknown where the style would have originated, but because this tradition was well preserved by the Corsicans, the name is upheld by them. There are similar sounds to this chant that can be heard in Georgian chant, Greek-Byzantine chant, Mozarabic chant and even Sri Lankan and Korean folk music. It is a universal sound of transcendence that Musica Dei hope to bring the cosmopolitan city of Manchester.
